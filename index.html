About



Ivaltiano was established by Ivalt Sello Sekhaolelo on 24/03/2024. The inspiration behind the establishment of the brand Ivaltiano came through designing a brand and Logo for another potential businessperson. “I had a version to establish a place where people will come and get 360 degrees Satisfying and fulfilling customer service, without feeling a need to go else to fill a void on a particular day. The dream vison is meet all daily needs of a customer at one place.



People do not mind spending; they just want to spend where they will see value for their expenditure”. Ivaltiano is aiming to meet the following customer services: Salon (Hair, Make-up, Nails), Spa (Massage, and related treatments), Designer clothing (Custom made fashion clothes), Restaurant (Serving variety of food and beverages at a classic level with good live music experience), conference halls ( Hosting events as per customer preference, e.g. Year End functions, Business Seminars, Birthday Parties, Indoor weddings etc.,), Butchery, Gaden Events (Catering outdoors weddings and parties, outdoor events, and Picnics), and a Lodge (After all these activities people need a place to rest with comfort and security).



Ivaltino target young adults, Middle age groups, and pensioners. This is for people who has the will and spending power. The service is classic, elegant and first class. Our target market is people who are from local, national, and International. It’s not easy to discern people, however cleanliness, morality and discipline, responsibility are the core values of our business.



Every time I came across something that looked like my vision I got steered up and became eager to achieve this goal. I got the motivation from places like Max’s Lifestyle in Durban South Africa, Indaba Hotel, Spa rand Conference Center. Growing up from a family where there is no legacy nor Investments, and the desire to live free from a 9-5 lifestyle is one inspiration amongst others.



The Aim is to give people a sense of belonging, meeting the physical, mental, Emotional, and spiritual needs. Bring people together for a good purpose, inspire growth and development. Addressing Issues of Unemployment amongst surrounding communities and providing people with quality and valuable skills they can use to enhance themselves.



Our operation will be at one specific place and will expand to another when there is a huge need to fill the gap. Being at one place keeps the spark and the and originality of the establishment.



Our Marketing strategy will be through social media, Business website and a word of Mouth.



We would like to categorize our business as unique through our standard of operation, quality of service, and our values; therefore, our competition is us.
